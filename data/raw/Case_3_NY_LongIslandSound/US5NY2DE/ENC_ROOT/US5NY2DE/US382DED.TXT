COLREGS: 33 CFR 80.160a

The Inland Navigational Rules Act of 1980 is in effect for vessels transiting this area. The seaward boundaries of this area are the COLREGS demarcation lines. In the are seaward of the COLREGS demarcation lines, vessels are governed by COLREGS: International Regulations for Preventing Collisions ate Sea, 1972. The COLREGS demarcation lines are defined in 33 CFR 80.160a.
