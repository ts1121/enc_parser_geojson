PRECAUTIONARY AREA

Recommended traffic lanes have been
established for the approaches to
Narragansett Bay and Buzzards Bay:
Use ENC US3NY01M and US4MA23M.

Traffic within the Precautionary Area
may consist of vessels operating
between Narragansett Bay and one
of the established traffic lanes. Mari-
ners are advised to exercise extreme
care in navigating within this area.
